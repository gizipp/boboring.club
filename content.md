  Execution Tips for TikTok/Reels

  Technical:
  - Vertical format (9:16)
  - Hook in first 1-3 seconds
  - Use trending sounds when relevant (but your own music works too!)
  - Text overlays for sound-off viewers
  - Keep it 7-15 seconds (shorter = better completion rate = more reach)

  Visual Style:
  - Simple, clean, not overly edited
  - Show real toddlers engaging with content (with parent permission)
  - B-roll of your actual videos playing
  - Mix of lifestyle + screen recordings

  Posting Strategy:
  - Post 1-2x daily to start
  - Best times: 6-9am, 12-2pm, 7-10pm (when parents are scrolling)
  - Use 5-8 hashtags, mix of broad + niche
  - Reply to every comment in first hour

  CTAs that Work:
  - "link in bio for full video"
  - "boboring.club → youtube + spotify"
  - "welcome to the club 💛 link in bio"
  - Keep it simple and consistent
