Our story

boboring.club is for parents who need screen time sometimes—but don’t want the hyper-fast, hyper-bright, hyper-loud stuff that leaves kids wired and cranky.

The anti-cocomelon philosophy

  • Low stimulus visuals — slower pacing, calmer color, fewer cuts.
  • Happy music — catchy, warm, and made for repeat listens.
  • Big emotions — gentle storytelling that helps kids feel and name feelings.

What we’re building

A growing library of music and parent-friendly resources: transition guides, low-stimulus explanations, and printables that make “purposeful screen time” easier.
